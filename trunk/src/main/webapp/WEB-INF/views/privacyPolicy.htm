A cookie is a small file placed onto your device. Cookies enable LinkAlma to identify your device, and enable the functioning of many LinkAlma features, including your ability to log into your account.

If you don’t want to receive cookies, you can change your browser settings. If you use LinkAlma without changing your browser settings, we'll assume that you’re happy to receive all cookies on the LinkAlma website. Please note that the LinkAlma site will not work properly without cookies.

 

What time cookies are put on your device?

Cookies may be set by LinkAlma when you visit LinkAlma.com, or they may be set by other websites or services who run content on the page you’re viewing (known as third-party cookies).

You can also learn more about LinkAlma’s advertising practices by reading our Privacy Policy.

Cookies can be used to do lots of different things, like recognize you when you navigate to LinkAlma’s site, storing your preferences and improving your experience. Additionally, cookies allow LinkAlma to bring you advertising both on and off the LinkAlma site, and bring customized features to you through LinkAlma plugins such as our share button.

 

LinkAlma uses cookies for a number of purposes, including:

 

Authentication– LinkAlma uses cookies to recognize you if you are logged in to LinkAlma, so that we can show you the right information and personalize your experience.

Security– LinkAlma uses cookies to support or enable security features we have deployed, and to help us detect malicious activity and violations of our User Agreement.

Advertising– LinkAlma may use cookies to show you relevant advertising both on and off the LinkAlma site.. Cookies and ad technology such as web beacons, pixels, and anonymous ad network tags help LinkAlma more effectively serve ads to you, as well as to collect aggregated auditing, research, and reporting for advertisers. Note: Because your web browser may request advertisements and web beacons directly from ad network servers, these networks can view, edit, or set their own cookies, just as if you had requested a web page from their site.

Performance, Analytics and Research– LinkAlma uses cookies to help us learn how well our site and plugins perform.
